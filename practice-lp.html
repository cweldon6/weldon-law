Life Planning (POA & Advance Directive)

Authority and clarity—so decisions can be made when they’re needed most.

What is Life Planning?

Life Planning puts in-life decision-making tools in place—so if you can’t act, the right person can help you immediately, without court delays.

  • Durable Financial Power of Attorney
  • Healthcare Power of Attorney / Proxy
  • Advance Directive / Living Will
  • HIPAA Authorization

Documents are tailored to your preferences and coordinated with your financial and medical providers where appropriate.

Why it matters

Properly executed POA and healthcare directives can avoid guardianship, reduce family conflict, and ensure your wishes are followed.

Who should be my agent?

Choose someone you trust, who is organized, and who is willing to follow your instructions.

Can I limit powers?

Yes—powers can be tailored, springing, or time-limited based on your needs.
