Long-Term Care Planning

Smart preparation to protect assets and ensure care when it’s needed most.

Our Approach

Nursing home and assisted living costs can quickly erode a lifetime of savings. Long-term care planning focuses on strategies to maintain eligibility for Medicaid while protecting assets for spouses and families.

  • Medicaid eligibility and application assistance
  • Asset preservation strategies
  • Spousal protections
  • Coordination with financial and healthcare providers

Why act early?

Timing matters. Early planning can expand your options, reduce stress, and allow for structured asset transfers within Medicaid look-back rules.

What if care is already needed?

We assist with crisis planning, identifying immediate options to protect what you can while securing care.

Can I protect my home?

Yes, there are strategies to preserve a primary residence for a spouse or heirs while qualifying for Medicaid.
