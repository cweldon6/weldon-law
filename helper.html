Weldon Law, LLC is a neighborhood-rooted law practice offering experienced, compassionate legal guidance in guardianship, estate planning, probate, long-term care planning and other elder law matters throughout New Jersey.

Founded by Christopher R. Weldon, Esquire—a lifelong Camden County resident and former State Regulatory Attorney—the firm brings over a decade of public service experience to the private sector. Chris has been involved in more than 1,000 guardianship and/or fiduciary cases, advocating for vulnerable individuals and those who support them.
