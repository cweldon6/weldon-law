Privacy & Terms

No Attorney–Client Relationship: Submitting information through this site, including the contact or intake forms, does not create an attorney–client relationship. Do not send confidential details until we have confirmed representation in writing.

Information We Collect

  • Details you provide in forms (name, email, phone number, type of matter, brief summary).
  • Basic technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device information (used for site security and functionality).
  • Anonymous analytics and traffic data to help us understand usage and improve the site.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry and evaluate whether we can assist you.
  • To schedule consultations and communicate with you.
  • To maintain website functionality and improve user experience.

We do not sell or rent personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers (e.g., website hosting, email delivery, scheduling platforms) only as needed to operate the site.

Cookies & Analytics

This site may use cookies or similar technologies for functionality and traffic measurement. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function correctly.

Third-Party Services

Form submissions may be processed through secure third-party platforms (such as Microsoft or Power Automate) to route inquiries. These services are contractually required to safeguard your data, but Weldon Law, LLC cannot guarantee their systems.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information submitted through the site, but no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. Submitting information is at your own risk.
