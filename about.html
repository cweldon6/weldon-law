About Weldon Law, LLC

Experienced, compassionate advocacy for New Jersey families.

Christopher R. Weldon, Esquire—founder of Weldon Law—has dedicated his legal career to protecting the rights, dignity, and independence of older adults and individuals with special needs. He focuses on guardianship; life & legacy planning; long‑term care preparation; probate matters; and related elder‑law issues.

With over a decade of elder‑law experience and involvement in more than 1,000 guardianship and fiduciary matters, Chris is known for a compassionate, steady approach and for resolving conflict efficiently. His background includes service as a State Regulatory Attorney with the New Jersey Office of the Public Guardian, legislative advocacy to improve elder‑care and guardianship laws, and frequent presentations to judicial and professional audiences.
