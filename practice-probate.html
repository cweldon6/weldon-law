Guidance through estate administration

Losing a loved one is difficult. We help executors and families navigate probate and estate administration with clarity and compassion—from Surrogate’s Court filings to asset transfers and final accounting.

  • Probate of Will / intestate administration
  • Notices to heirs and beneficiaries
  • Inventory, valuation, and debt resolution
  • Tax filings and final distributions

Each matter moves at a different pace depending on assets, beneficiaries, and any disputes. We’ll outline a practical timeline and keep you informed throughout.

Common questions

Do all estates have to go through probate?

Not always. Small estates or assets with beneficiary designations can transfer outside probate. We’ll help you determine what’s required.

How long does probate take?

Simple estates can wrap up in months; more complex estates may take longer. We’ll set expectations early and keep things moving.

What if there’s a dispute?

We work to resolve issues efficiently, from clarifying fiduciary duties to addressing objections, always aiming to minimize stress and cost.
