About Weldon Law

Weldon Law delivers a modern, community-rooted approach to elder law—making complex legal issues clearer, decisions less overwhelming, and solutions more practical for families across New Jersey.

Every matter is handled with compassion, clarity, and efficiency so that clients and their loved ones feel supported through some of life’s most difficult transitions.

Christopher R. Weldon, Esq., the firm’s founder, brings more than a decade of public service experience to the private sector, including involvement in over 1,000 guardianship and fiduciary matters statewide.

By combining that background with modern technology and streamlined workflows, Weldon Law provides steady advocacy, responsive communication, and tailored strategies—ensuring every case receives the attention it deserves from start to finish.

  • Transparent process and timely communication

  • Local expertise with statewide experience
  • Technology-enabled efficiency without losing the personal touch

Chris brings over a decade of focused experience in elder law matters, with deep knowledge of healthcare regulations, benefit programs, and the complex intersection of legal and financial planning for aging adults.
